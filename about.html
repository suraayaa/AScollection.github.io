About AS Collection

AS Collection is a prominent fashion brand company based in Penang, Malaysia. Inspired by the success and global appeal of brands like H&M and UNIQLO, AS Collection strives to deliver affordable, trendy, and high-quality fashion products to its customers. The company aims to create a unique shopping experience that caters to the diverse fashion needs of individuals while staying committed to sustainability and ethical practices. AS Collection also makes it possible for customers around the world to express themselves through fashion and design and to choose a more sustainable lifestyle. We create value for people and society in general by delivering our customer offering and by developing with a focus on sustainable and profitable growth. Young people who have a casual dress sense, who shun stereotypes, and who want to feel good in whatever they are wearing. To meet their needs, AS Collection takes the latest international trends, mixing them with the influences that are seen on the street and in the most fashionable clubs, and reworks them according to their style, thus turning them into comfortable and easy-to-wear garments. AS Collection evolves at the same pace as its customers, always watching out for new technologies, social movements, and the latest artistic or musical trends. All of this can be seen reflected not only in its designs but also in stores.
